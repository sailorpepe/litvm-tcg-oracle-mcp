## Why BSL?

- **Auditable**: Anyone can read and verify the oracle logic, the simulation math, and the Merkle proof construction. No hidden code.
- **Transparent**: All parameters, calibration methods, and data sources are documented and verifiable on-chain.
- **Protected**: The business is sustainable. Nobody can fork and commercially compete for 4 years.
- **Eventually free**: After 4 years, every version automatically becomes MIT — fully open source forever.
- **Community-friendly**: Research, education, plugins, security audits — all explicitly free.

This is the same license model used by HashiCorp (Terraform), MariaDB, CockroachDB, and Sentry.
